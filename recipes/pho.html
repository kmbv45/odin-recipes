Chicken Pho Recipe


Ingredients

Charred Aromatics

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 Onions
  • 2" piece of ginger

Soup Broth

  • 2 quarts water
  • 3 pounds chicken thighs
  • 5 star anise pods
  • 1 cinammon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds
  • 8 tsp fish sauce
  • 8 tsp chicken powder
  • 13 ounces dried rice noodles

Cooking Instructions

  1. char your aromatics until blackened
  2. combine soup broth ingredients in large pot
  3. bring soup broth to a boil
  4. add charred aromatics
  5. cook broth for 1.5 hours
  6. add dry noodles to broth for last 10 min of cook time
  7. put in bowl and serve
