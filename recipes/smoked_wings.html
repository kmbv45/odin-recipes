Smoked Chicken Wings


Ingredients

  • 2 pounds raw party wings
  • 1/2 cup Honey Hog Rub
  • 1 tbs of olive oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. pat wings completely dry
  2. toss wings in bowl with oil
  3. add honey hog rub and toss in bowl
  4. set smoker to 500 degrees
  5. add wings to smoker
  6. cook for 15 min, then flip
  7. finish cooking to internal temp 200 degrees
  8. serve
